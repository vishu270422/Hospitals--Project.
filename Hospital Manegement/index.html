About Us

NARINDER MOHAN HOSPITAL & HEART CENTRE is a charitable institution founded by Late Padma Shri NARINDER NATH MOHAN, who was the Managing Director of Mohan Meakins Limited. Construction of the hospital commenced in 1969 and the hospital started functioning in the year 1974. The institution including residential complex is spread in an area of approximately 17 acres. The hospital is located at Mohan Nagar, Ghaziabad on G.T. Road near the eastern border of Delhi and the nearest metro station is Mohan Nagar. The Hospital is certified by the National Accreditation Board for Hospitals & Healthcare Providers (NABH) and National Accreditation Board for Testing and Calibration Laboratories (NABL) Accredited!

Emergency

For accidents and emergencies, the Casualty Department, is located strategically at the entry of the hospital. It is manned, round the clock by Casualty Medical Officers backed up by specialists doctors on call from each discipline, nurses and para-medical staff. It is equipped with its own X-ray machine, ECG machine, equipment for cardiac monitoring, operation theatre and emergency medicines. The department is backed up by the services of Ambulance, Laboratory and Blood Bank round the clock. Medico-Legal cases are also being attended.!

Surgical Treatment

All types of surgeries, both minor & major are undertaken in the hospital. E.g General Surgical Urological Gynaecological & Obstetrical Plastic Surgery Neuro Surgery Orthopaedic Surgery Arthoroscopy / Joint Replacement Endoscopic surgeries
